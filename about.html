SCrypto. International

SCrypto International is a secure, reliable and advanced digital asset database platform developed for international customers and built on SCrypto's cutting-edge technology. This international library platform provides innovative tokens a more streamlined approach for being listed, while also providing instant trade reports, dependable digital wallets, and industry-leading security practices.

Mission & Vision

We are committed to being a driving force in the blockchain revolution by increasing adoption of this innovative technology around the world. Using our streamlined token listing process, we will advance new, innovative blockchain projects that could potentially transform the way goods, services and operations are managed globally.
